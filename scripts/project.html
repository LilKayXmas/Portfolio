E-Mentoring

For this project, students were given a well-know company advisor.

The advisor would ask the student to find a way to help improve an aspect of the company.

I was tasked with Wells Fargo. I was to help the board find a better way to connect with their employees in the different branches.

  • Students will contact their advisor to understand what they are suppose to be tasked with
  • Students will keep a journal of every meeting
  • Students make a PowerPoint that contains their case study
  • Students will record or have advisor record their presentation
